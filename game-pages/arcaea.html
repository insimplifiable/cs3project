Arcaea was released in both iOS and Android on the 9th of March, 2017, and then on Nintendo Switch on the 18th of May, 2021. As one of the most notable mobile rhythm games, it expresses and adds the idea of 3D perspective in scrolling, wherein notes on a 3D space come towards a 2D plane, rather than the general 2D perspective (some compatible for PC rhythm games), where notes on a 2D plane come towards the 'line'.
Because of this, apart from the main objects in scroll rhythm games, it introduces other mechanics that utilize such an idea, adding more creativity in charts. But, beware if you wish to be a free-to-play player, since majority of the content is very much hid under a paywall made for the creator's funding of the game.

Background

As of the current update (4.1.4), the menu features 4 main sections of the game: Music Play, World Mode, Story Mode, and Course Mode, each with different functions. They all use and give the player some of the game's main currency, fragments, and other items like cores and crystals. But, most content are to be paid with memories, the paid currency of the game, hence its unpleasantness towards free-to-play players.
