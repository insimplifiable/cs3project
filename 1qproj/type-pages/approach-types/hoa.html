Helping Object Approach (HOA)

Unlike the MOA type, these games revolve around another object instead to approach towards the object of input. Usually, once it is perfectly in the middle or on the border of the object, it is the perfect time to perform necessary input, and is dependent on the game's settings.
Again, there may be instances of performing other actions to the object if it is a different type, both inputwise and actionwise.
